About

Startup Simulation is a 3 day event hosted by the Asian Business Students Association and the Management Information Systems Association as an initiative to provide an opportunity for students to design and create a start-up plan. This is where you can start turning your ideas into a reality. You'll make new friends and learn new skills with the help of industry professionals!

Come down for a weekend of food, workshops, mentorships, & prizes. We would love to see you there!

FAQ

What's Startup Simulation?

Startup Simulation is a fusion between Case Competition and Hackathon, where you create a idea / business plan to solve a problem. You take care of building the idea. We'll take care of you.

How do teams work?

Teams have to be 3 - 4 people. You can sign up as a group or individually. Individuals signing up solo will be randomly assigned into a team. After signing up please venmo $10 (each person) to @TexasABSA as deposit. We will return the deposit at the end of the competition.

Who can attend?

Startup Simulation is open to all UT Students!

More Questions?

Feel free to contact us at startupsimulation2018@gmail.com at any time!

I’ve never been to a case competition like event and I’m nervous.

Don't be! Startup Simulation has beginner workshops to help you get started and running. We also assign you an industry professional as a mentor on the second day to help you with your idea!

What's the event schedule?

Click here to see the event schedule. Startup Simulation will start at 5 PM on Friday, November 16th and end on Sunday.

What should I bring?

Student ID, Laptop, Charger, and an extension cord (recommended).
