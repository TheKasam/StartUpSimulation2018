About

StartUp Simulation is a 3 day event hosted by the Asian Business Student Association and the Management Information Systems Association as an initiative to provide an oppertunity for students to think about and create a start-up plan. This is where you can start turning your ideas into a reality. You'll make new friends and learn new skills with the help of industry professionals!

Come down for a weekend of food, workshops, mentorships, & prizes. We would love to see you there!

FAQ

What's StartUp Simulation?

StartUp Simulation is a case competition like event where you create a idea / business plan to slove a problem You take care of building the idea. We'll take care of you.

How do teams work?

Teams have to be 3 - 4 people. You can also check out our facebook group for attendees.

Who can attend?

The event is open to all UT Students!

More Questions?

Feel free to contact us at startupsimulation2018@gmail.com at any time!

Ive never been to a case compeittion like event and Im nervous.

Don't be! StartUp Simulator has beginner workshops to help you get up and running. We also assign you an industry professional as a mentor to meet up with the second day to help you with your idea!
